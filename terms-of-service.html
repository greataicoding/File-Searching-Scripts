2. Description of Service

File Search & Auditor for Google Drive™ is a Google Workspace™ Add-on that searches your Google Drive™ and Shared Drives for files matching a search string, and reports the results — including each file's sharing permissions — to a Google Sheets™ spreadsheet. It runs entirely within your own Google account using Google Apps Script™.

3. Your Responsibilities

You are solely responsible for:

  • Reviewing the results carefully before taking any action in Google Drive based on them.
  • Setting the ORG_DOMAIN constant in the script correctly if you want the Risk Flag column to accurately reflect external sharing for your organisation. While ORG_DOMAIN is blank, the Risk Flag fires on every named user/group permission.
  • Ensuring you have appropriate authorisation to use this add-on within your organisation if you are using a Google Workspace™ account, particularly when auditing Shared Drives that contain content owned by other people.
