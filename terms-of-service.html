2. Description of Service

The App is a Google Workspace™ Add-on that searches your Google Drive™ and Shared Drives for files matching a search string, and reports the results — including each file's sharing permissions — to a Google Sheets™ spreadsheet. It runs entirely within your own Google account using Google Apps Script™.

  • Setting the ORG_DOMAIN constant in the script correctly if you want the Risk Flag column to accurately reflect external sharing for your organisation. While ORG_DOMAIN is blank, the Risk Flag fires on every named user/group permission.
