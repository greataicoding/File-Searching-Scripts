Drive Auditor

Privacy & Data

Drive Auditor does not transmit, store, or share any data outside of your Google account. All searching, processing, and reporting happens entirely within Google Apps Script™ — there are no external servers, no databases, and no third-party services involved. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
