CUSTOMIZATION NOTES
================================================================================

The generator script is fully modular and commented. To customize:

1. Change Colours:
   Edit hex values at the top of drive_auditor_icon_generator.py
   (CREAM_BG, BROWN_DARK, STEEL_GREY, etc.)

2. Add New Icons:
   Create a new draw_my_icon(draw, size) function
   Add it to the icons{} dictionary in export_icons()

3. Modify Details:
   • Adjust rivet size: change rivet_size parameter in draw_rivets()
   • Adjust weathering: change density in draw_weathering_marks()
   • Adjust shadow: change shadow_offset and shadow_opacity in apply_subtle_shadow()

4. Batch Regeneration:
   python3 drive_auditor_icon_generator.py
   (Change output_dir in __main__ to your preferred location)
