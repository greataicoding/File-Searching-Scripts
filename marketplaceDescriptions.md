Short Description
Search Google Drive™ by content and audit who has access to every file found.

Long Description
Drive™ Auditor searches every Google Shared Drive™ you have access to (and your personal My Drive™) for files matching any string — and tells you exactly who has access to each one.

Unlike Google Drive™ native search, Drive™ Auditor gives you a permanent, sortable, filterable record of what you searched for and what came back. Every search creates a new tab in your Google Sheets™ spreadsheet — so you can come back days later, remember what you were looking for, and act on the results.

KEY FEATURES

- Cross-Drive™ search — auto-discovers every Shared Drive™ you can access, then lets you tick which ones to include in each search
- Full-text content search — finds files by what's inside them, not just their titles. Use "exact phrases" or title:keyword for precision
- Files, folders, or both — pick what you want to find with simple checkboxes
- Permission auditing — every result lists the named users and groups with access to each file
- External sharing detection — files shared with "anyone with the link" or with users outside your organisation are automatically flagged
- Persistent search history — every search lives in its own spreadsheet tab, with frozen headers and column filters ready to use
- One-click file access — open any result directly in Drive™ to review or update its sharing settings

PERFECT FOR

- Tidying up after departed contractors or ex-employees
- Auditing what's been shared with personal Gmail™ addresses
- Finding files you remember by content but not by title
- Compliance reviews and access audits
- Locating that one document someone swears exists somewhere in the company drives

PRIVACY

Drive Auditor runs entirely inside your own Google™ account using Google Apps Script™. No data is transmitted, stored, or shared outside your account. Read-only access — Drive™ Auditor cannot modify, move, or delete any file. Full source code and privacy policy available at the website link.
